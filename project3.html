Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a budding enthusiast, our comprehensive guides will help you grow a thriving garden. From vegetables and fruits to herbs and flowers, we cover a wide variety of plants with detailed instructions on planting, care, and harvesting.

Tomato Overview: A versatile fruit used in many dishes. Growing Conditions: Full sun; 70-85°F; well-drained, loamy soil; consistent moisture. Planting: Start seeds indoors 6-8 weeks before the last frost; transplant outdoors. Care: Prune suckers, fertilize every 2-3 weeks, monitor for pests. Harvest: When fully colored and slightly soft.

Basil Overview: Fragrant herb commonly used in cooking. Growing Conditions: Full sun; 70-90°F; well-drained soil; keep soil moist. Planting: Sow seeds directly after the last frost or start indoors. Care: Pinch off top leaves for bushy growth, use balanced fertilizer. Harvest: Leaves as needed, avoiding more than a third at a time.

Carrot Overview: Root vegetable, rich in vitamins. Growing Conditions: Full sun to partial shade; 60-70°F; loose, sandy soil; even moisture. Planting: Sow seeds directly 2-4 weeks before the last frost; thin seedlings. Care: Thin seedlings, use low-nitrogen fertilizer. Harvest: When roots are 1/2 to 1 inch in diameter.

Mint Overview: Aromatic herb used in cooking and beverages. Growing Conditions: Full sun to partial shade; 55-70°F; moist, well-drained soil. Planting: Sow seeds directly after the last frost or start indoors. Care: Regular pruning, monthly balanced fertilizer. Harvest: Leaves as needed, best before flowering.

Lettuce Overview: Cool-weather leafy vegetable. Growing Conditions: Full sun to partial shade; 60-65°F; moist, rich soil. Planting: Sow seeds directly 2-4 weeks before the last frost; thin seedlings. Care: Thin seedlings, use balanced fertilizer. Harvest: Leaves as needed, starting with the outer leaves.

Strawberry Overview: Sweet red fruits, popular in desserts. Growing Conditions: Full sun; 60-80°F; well-drained, sandy loam soil; consistent moisture. Planting: Plant bare roots in early spring; space plants 12-18 inches apart. Care: Use straw mulch, balanced fertilizer at planting and post-harvest. Harvest: When berries are fully red and slightly soft.

Zucchini Overview: Versatile summer squash, abundant yields. Growing Conditions: Full sun; 70-95°F; well-drained, fertile soil; consistent moisture. Planting: Sow seeds directly after the last frost; space plants 24-36 inches apart. Care: Thin seedlings, balanced fertilizer every 3-4 weeks. Harvest: When fruits are 6-8 inches long.

Lavender Overview: Aromatic herb, fragrant flowers. Growing Conditions: Full sun; 60-85°F; well-drained, sandy soil; dry between waterings. Planting: Start seeds indoors 8-10 weeks before the last frost; space plants 18-24 inches apart. Care: Annual pruning, low-nitrogen fertilizer once a year. Harvest: Flowers just as buds start to open.
